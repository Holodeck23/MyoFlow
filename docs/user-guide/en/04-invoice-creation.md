# Invoice Creation

This guide covers the process of creating and managing professional, Austrian-compliant invoices with MyoFlow.

## Creating an Invoice

To create a new invoice:

1.  Navigate to the "Invoices" section.
2.  Click the "New Invoice" button.
3.  Select the client and the service date. The date picker will not allow you to select a future date, ensuring compliance with Austrian regulations.

[Screenshot: New invoice form with date picker]

## Adding Invoice Line Items

For each invoice, you can add multiple line items. For each item, you will need to provide:

*   A description of the service or product.
*   The quantity.
*   The unit price.
*   The applicable VAT rate.

[Screenshot: Invoice line items table]

## VAT Rate Selection

MyoFlow supports various VAT statuses to suit your business needs:

*   **Kleinunternehmer:** For small businesses exempt from VAT.
*   **10%, 13%, and 20%:** Standard Austrian VAT rates.

## Sequential Invoice Numbering

MyoFlow automatically assigns a sequential invoice number in the Austrian standard format `YYYY-NNN` (e.g., `2025-001`), ensuring your records are organized and compliant.

## Downloading PDF Invoices

Once an invoice is finalized, you can generate a professional, Austrian-compliant PDF version for your records or to send to your clients. Simply click the "Download PDF" button on the invoice details page.

[Screenshot: Generated PDF invoice]

## Viewing Invoice History

The "Invoices" section provides a complete history of all your invoices, which can be searched and sorted by status, client, or amount.
