# Settings Configuration

This guide provides an overview of the various settings you can configure in MyoFlow to tailor the platform to your needs.

[Screenshot: Settings page with tabs]

## Profile

In the "Profile" tab, you can manage your core business information:

*   Business Name
*   Address
*   Email and Phone Number
*   UID Number (VAT Identification Number)
*   IBAN for bank transfers

[Screenshot: Profile settings form]

## Tax Compliance

The "Tax Compliance" tab helps you manage your tax obligations:

*   **Kleinunternehmer Status:** A toggle to indicate whether you are operating under the small business VAT exemption.
*   **Threshold Tracking:** MyoFlow tracks your annual revenue to help you monitor your Kleinunternehmer status.

[Screenshot: Tax compliance widget]

## Invoice Branding

Customize your invoices in the "Invoice Branding" tab:

*   **Logo Upload:** Add your business logo to your invoices.
*   **Thank-You Message:** Include a personalized message at the bottom of your invoices.

## System Preferences

Adjust the platform's behavior in the "System Preferences" tab:

*   **Language:** Switch between English and German.
*   **Timezone and Currency:** Set your preferred timezone and currency.
*   **Notification Toggles:** Manage your email notification preferences.

## Travel Settings

For therapists who travel to clients, the "Travel Settings" tab allows you to:

*   Set your base location.
*   Define your method of transport.
*   Configure your travel rates and distance limits.

## Pricing Templates

Streamline your invoicing process by creating service rate templates in the "Pricing Templates" tab. You can create, edit, and delete templates for your most common services.
