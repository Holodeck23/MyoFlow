# Appointment Scheduling

This guide explains how to use MyoFlow's calendar to manage your appointments, from scheduling new sessions to tracking their status.

## Calendar Navigation

The calendar provides a comprehensive view of all your scheduled appointments. You can navigate between months and view your schedule at a glance. The calendar also displays Austrian public holidays, so you can plan accordingly.

[Screenshot: Calendar view with appointments]

## Creating Appointments

To schedule a new appointment:

1.  Click on an empty time slot in the calendar or use the "New Appointment" button.
2.  In the appointment creation form, you will need to:
    *   Link the appointment to an existing client.
    *   Select the service to be provided.
    *   Set the date and time.
    *   Choose the location for the appointment.

[Screenshot: New appointment modal]

## Appointment Statuses

Appointments in MyoFlow follow a clear workflow, indicated by their status:

*   **BOOKED:** The appointment has been scheduled.
*   **COMPLETED:** The session has taken place.
*   **PAID:** The client has paid for the service.

[Screenshot: Appointment detail view]

## Multi-Location Appointments

MyoFlow supports therapists who work at multiple locations. When creating an appointment, you can select the appropriate location from a dropdown menu of your saved business addresses.

## Travel Buffers

To prevent scheduling conflicts and allow for travel time, MyoFlow automatically adds travel buffers between appointments scheduled at different locations.

## Conflict Warnings

The system will warn you if you attempt to schedule an appointment that overlaps with an existing one, helping you avoid double-bookings.
