# Client Management

This guide covers the essentials of managing your clients in MyoFlow, from adding new clients to keeping their information secure.

## Creating a New Client

To add a new client to your records:

1.  Navigate to the "Clients" section from the main menu.
2.  Click the "New Client" button.
3.  Fill out the client information form. The following fields are required for compliance with Austrian invoicing standards:
    *   Full Name
    *   Email
    *   Phone Number
    *   Full Mailing Address (Street, Postal Code, City, Country)

[Screenshot: New client form with address fields]

## Viewing a Client Profile

To view a client's profile, simply click on their name in the client list. The profile view displays all of the client's information, including their contact details, appointment history, and any notes you have added.

[Screenshot: Client profile view]

## Editing Client Information

You can update a client's information at any time by navigating to their profile and clicking the "Edit" button.

## Adding Client Notes

You can add timestamped notes to a client's record to keep track of important information.

1.  From the client's profile, go to the "Notes" section.
2.  Click "Add Note" to create a new entry.

## Deleting Clients

To delete a client, go to their profile and click the "Delete" button. A confirmation dialog will appear to prevent accidental deletions.

## Security Note

MyoFlow uses field-level encryption (via libsodium) to protect sensitive client data, such as health flags and medical notes, ensuring that this information remains confidential and secure.
