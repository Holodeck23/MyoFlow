# Getting Started with MyoFlow

Welcome to MyoFlow! This guide will walk you through the initial setup of your account, from signing up to navigating your dashboard.

## System Requirements

For the best experience with MyoFlow, we recommend using the latest version of one of the following web browsers:
- Google Chrome
- Mozilla Firefox

## Sign-Up Process

You can create a MyoFlow account using your email and a password, or by linking your existing Google account.

1.  Navigate to the MyoFlow sign-up page.
2.  Choose your preferred sign-up method:
    *   **Email and Password:** Fill in your email address and create a secure password.
    *   **Google OAuth:** Click the "Sign up with Google" button and follow the prompts to link your account.

[Screenshot: Sign-in page]

## Onboarding Wizard

After signing up, you will be guided through a 3-step onboarding wizard to set up your practice essentials.

*   **Step 1: Business Information:** Enter your business name, address, and contact details.
*   **Step 2: Professional Details:** Specify your professional designation (e.g., Heilmasseur) and any relevant qualifications.
*   **Step 3: Completion:** Review your information and complete the setup.

[Screenshot: Onboarding wizard - Step 1]

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is your central hub for managing your practice. Here you will find:

*   A personalized welcome message.
*   The **Profile Completion Widget**, which shows how much of your profile is complete and links to the settings page.
*   Quick navigation tiles for easy access to Clients, Appointments, and Invoices.

[Screenshot: Dashboard with profile completion widget]

## Language Switching

MyoFlow supports both English and German. You can switch between languages at any time using the language toggle located in the top-right corner of any page.
