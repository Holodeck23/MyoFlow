# Austrian Tax Compliance Guide for Therapists

This guide provides an overview of the key Austrian tax regulations relevant to therapists using MyoFlow.

## Kleinunternehmer (Small Business Exemption)

*   **What it is:** An exemption from collecting Value Added Tax (VAT) for businesses with low annual revenue.
*   **Eligibility:** Your annual gross revenue must be less than €55,000.
*   **Legal Basis:** §6 Abs. 1 Z 27 UStG (Austrian VAT Act).
*   **How it works in MyoFlow:**
    *   You can enable the Kleinunternehmer status in "Settings" > "Tax Compliance."
    *   MyoFlow will automatically track your annual revenue to help you monitor the threshold.
    *   When enabled, your invoices will include the legally required text: "Umsatzsteuerfrei gemäß §6 Abs. 1 Z 27 UStG."
*   **When to switch to VAT:** You should consider switching to standard VAT if you are approaching the €55,000 threshold or if you expect your revenue to exceed it.

## VAT Rates (Umsatzsteuer)

*   **10%:** The reduced rate, which applies to most therapy services, such as massage and physiotherapy.
*   **13%:** A reduced rate for specific services, including cultural and educational services.
*   **20%:** The standard VAT rate, which applies to products, consulting, and other standard services.
*   **How to select:** You can choose the appropriate VAT rate for each line item when creating an invoice.
*   **Invoice requirements:** If you are subject to VAT, your invoices must show the VAT amount separately.

## RKSV (Cash Register Obligation)

*   **Threshold:** The RKSV applies to businesses with an annual revenue exceeding €15,000.
*   **Requirement:** Businesses subject to the RKSV must use an electronic receipt system (Belegerteilungspflicht).
*   **Status in MyoFlow:** ⚠️ **Coming Soon** - MyoFlow has schema support for RKSV, but the feature is not yet fully implemented.
*   **Recommendation:** We strongly recommend consulting a tax advisor when your annual revenue approaches the €15,000 threshold to ensure you remain compliant.

## Professional Designations

MyoFlow supports the following official Austrian massage therapy designations:

*   **HEILMASSEUR:** A medical masseur, recognized as a healthcare provider.
*   **MEDIZINISCHER_MASSEUR:** A medical massage therapist.
*   **GEWERBLICHER_MASSEUR:** A commercial masseur, operating under the Gewerbeordnung (trade regulation).

The choice of designation is important as it can affect your tax treatment and eligibility for insurance billing.
