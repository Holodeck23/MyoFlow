# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account & Setup
**Q: How do I sign up for MyoFlow?**
A: You can sign up using your email and a password, or by linking your Google account.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: MyoFlow is available in German (Austria) and English.

**Q: Can I change my language preference?**
A: Yes, you can switch the language at any time using the toggle in the top-right corner of any page.

**Q: What do I need to do during the onboarding wizard?**
A: The 3-step wizard guides you through setting up your essential business and professional details.

## Clients
**Q: Why do I need to enter my clients' full addresses?**
A: A complete client address is a legal requirement for Austrian invoice compliance.

**Q: How is my client data secured?**
A: Sensitive client data, like health notes, is protected with field-level encryption using libsodium.

**Q: Can I delete a client?**
A: Yes, you can delete a client from their profile page. A confirmation dialog will prevent accidental deletion.

**Q: Where can I add notes about a client?**
A: You can add timestamped notes within each client's profile.

## Invoices
**Q: Why can't I select a future service date on an invoice?**
A: Austrian law requires that invoices are issued for services that have already been provided. The date picker prevents selecting future dates to ensure compliance.

**Q: What invoice numbering format does MyoFlow use?**
A: Invoices are numbered sequentially in the format `YYYY-NNN` (e.g., `2025-001`), as is standard in Austria.

**Q: How do I download a PDF of an invoice?**
A: You can download the PDF by clicking the "Download" button on the invoice detail page.

**Q: Can I create an invoice for a client who hasn't had an appointment?**
A: Yes, you can create invoices for any client, independent of the appointment calendar.

## Tax & Compliance
**Q: What is the "Kleinunternehmer" (Small Business) regulation?**
A: It is an Austrian tax rule that exempts businesses with an annual gross revenue below €55,000 from collecting VAT.

**Q: When should I use VAT rates on my invoices?**
A: You should apply VAT if your annual revenue exceeds the €55,000 Kleinunternehmer threshold, or if you have voluntarily opted to be a VAT-paying business.

**Q: What's the difference between the 10%, 13%, and 20% VAT rates?**
A: The correct rate depends on the type of service or product. Most therapy services fall under the 10% rate. Please consult our Austrian Tax Guide or a tax advisor for details.

**Q: Does MyoFlow handle RKSV (Cash Register Obligation)?**
A: Not yet. RKSV compliance features are planned for a future update. We recommend consulting a tax advisor if your revenue approaches the €15,000 threshold.

## Settings
**Q: Where can I add my business logo to invoices?**
A: You can upload your logo in "Settings" → "Invoice Branding".

**Q: How do I set up my travel rates?**
A: Configure your base location, transport method, and rates in "Settings" → "Travel".

**Q: What does the "profile completion" widget on the dashboard mean?**
A: It shows the percentage of required business information fields you have filled out in your profile settings. A complete profile is necessary for compliant invoicing.

**Q: Can I change my professional designation?**
A: Yes, you can update your professional title (e.g., Heilmasseur) in "Settings" → "Profile".
