# MyoFlow Landing Page Blueprint (Beta)

## Objectives
- Convert Austrian massage therapists and bodyworkers who currently juggle spreadsheets and manual compliance tasks.
- Communicate that MyoFlow is in open beta: users get full functionality with active feedback loop and rapid iterations.
- Capture leads who prefer a guided onboarding call alongside self-serve trial starts.

## Page Flow
1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline: “Run Your Austrian Therapy Practice Without Paperwork (Now in Beta).”
   - Subheadline: Emphasise calmer workdays, automated compliance, all-in-one operations.
   - Primary CTA: “Start 30-Day Beta Trial.” Secondary CTA: “Watch 2-Min Demo.”
   - Trust bar: “Built with Austrian therapists” + soft testimonial snippet.

2. **Problem → Solution Split**
   - Left column: highlight pains (manual RKSV tracking, invoice formatting, endless paperwork).
   - Right column: show dashboard hero mockup with calm colour palette; copy ties pain to solution.
   - Bullet list avoids hype—focus on tangible outcomes observed during beta (hours saved, compliance reminders).

3. **Feature Pillars (Three Cards)**
   1. Compliance Autopilot – tax thresholds, audit logs, RKSV reminders.
   2. Therapy Operations – encrypted notes, scheduling, travel calculation.
   3. Business Insights – exports to BMD/DATEV, revenue dashboards, intake forms.
   - Each card includes a short benefit-driven paragraph and optional lightweight animation loop.

4. **Day-in-the-Life Demo Flow**
   - Timeline (intake → booking → session notes → invoice → accountant export).
   - CTA below: “See Full Workflow” linking to demo video or interactive tour.

5. **Testimonials & Social Proof**
   - 2–3 quotes from beta therapists; include name + region.
   - Add “Beta Community” badge highlighting ongoing feature releases and feedback channel.

6. **Pricing Overview**
   - Present Beta-friendly tiers (Solo, Team) with note that pricing may adjust post-beta.
   - CTA buttons link to signup or contact sales for larger practices.

7. **Security & Compliance Assurance**
   - Checklist: Field-level encryption, GDPR alignment, Austrian hosting partners.
   - Link to security whitepaper and audit log explanation.

8. **Integrations & Compatibility**
   - Logos: BMD, DATEV, Google Calendar, Stripe (if applicable).
   - Short copy on effortless accountant handoff.

9. **FAQ / Objection Handling**
   - Sample Qs: “What does beta mean for support?”, “Is my data safe?”, “Can I export if I leave?”
   - Provide transparent answers, stress ongoing updates and roadmap visibility.

10. **Final Conversion Strip**
    - Reiterate beta value proposition: “Join the therapists shaping MyoFlow.”
    - Primary CTA repeat + secondary “Book Onboarding Call”.
    - Include contact email, support chat link, and regulatory disclosures.

## Visual Strategy
- Palette: calming greens/blues with warm accent CTA; airy whitespace.
- Typography: Inter for body, weighty display for headings.
- Imagery: Austrian therapy spaces, practitioners with clients, interface overlays.
- Micro-interactions: gentle hover states, scroll-triggered fades.

## Copy Guidelines
- Maintain professional, empathetic tone; avoid unverified claims (use “helps reduce”, “designed to”).
- Reinforce beta messaging: invite feedback, mention rapid iteration cycle.
- Emphasise Austrian localisation throughout (currency, language examples, compliance terms).
