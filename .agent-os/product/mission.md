# Product Mission

## Pitch

MyoFlow is an Austrian therapy practice management system that helps massage therapists replace fragmented Excel/Word/WhatsApp workflows by providing an all-in-one cockpit with regulatory compliance and revenue optimization features.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Solo Austrian Massage Therapists**: Individual practitioners (Heilmasseur, Medizinischer Masseur, Gewerblicher Masseur) currently using Excel-based workflows
- **Small Therapy Clinics**: 2-5 therapist practices needing shared client management and appointment coordination

### User Personas

**Maria, Solo Heilmasseur** (35-50 years old)
- **Role:** Independent Massage Therapist
- **Context:** Runs practice from home/small clinic, manages 20-40 clients, handles own admin work
- **Pain Points:** Excel crashes lose client data, manual invoice creation takes hours, VAT compliance confusion, missed appointment follow-ups
- **Goals:** Spend more time with clients, ensure legal compliance, reduce administrative overhead, increase client retention

**Thomas, Clinic Owner** (45-60 years old)
- **Role:** Practice Owner with 3 Therapists  
- **Context:** Manages team scheduling, client relationships, and business finances
- **Pain Points:** Double-booked appointments, inconsistent client notes, manual tax reporting, no client retention insights
- **Goals:** Streamline operations, improve team coordination, maximize revenue per client, scale practice efficiently

## The Problem

### Fragmented Administrative Workflows

Austrian massage therapists currently juggle Excel spreadsheets for client data, Word documents for invoices, WhatsApp for appointments, and paper forms for notes. This creates data silos, increases error risk, and wastes 2-3 hours daily on administrative tasks.

**Our Solution:** Unified digital cockpit with integrated client management, appointment scheduling, and Austrian-compliant invoicing.

### Regulatory Compliance Complexity

Austrian therapists must navigate GDPR Article 9 (health data), Kleinunternehmerregelung thresholds, VAT rates, and RKSV requirements. Many operate in legal gray areas due to complexity.

**Our Solution:** Built-in compliance frameworks with automated VAT calculations, legal notice generation, and audit-ready data protection.

### Revenue Leakage and Client Churn

Without systematic follow-up, therapists lose 20-30% of clients annually and miss upselling opportunities for package deals or wellness programs.

**Our Solution:** Automated reactivation campaigns, retention analytics, and revenue optimization features.

## Differentiators

### Austrian Regulatory Expertise

Unlike generic practice management tools, we provide Austria-specific compliance (Kleinunternehmerregelung, 9 Bundesländer holidays, therapist designations, RKSV awareness). This results in audit-ready operations and reduced legal risk.

### Therapist-First UX Design

While competitors focus on large clinics, we optimize for solo practitioners who wear multiple hats. Our German-first interface and simplified workflows reduce training time from weeks to hours.

### Revenue Optimization Focus

Beyond basic scheduling and invoicing, we provide client lifetime value analytics, automated retention campaigns, and package deal management to increase practice profitability by 15-25%.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **Client Management with Health Data Protection:** GDPR-compliant client profiles with encrypted health notes and audit logging
- **Austrian-Compliant Invoicing:** Automated VAT calculations, Kleinunternehmer support, sequential numbering, and PDF generation
- **Appointment Scheduling with Holiday Support:** All 9 Bundesländer holidays, conflict detection, and automated reminders
- **PDF Invoice Generation:** Professional Austrian-format invoices with Puppeteer rendering and email integration

### Business Optimization Features

- **Service Rate Management:** Default pricing templates with package deal support and seasonal adjustments
- **Revenue Analytics Dashboard:** Client lifetime value tracking, retention metrics, and profitability insights
- **Automated Follow-up Campaigns:** Reactivation emails, birthday greetings, and wellness check-ins
- **CSV Accounting Exports:** BMD/RZL/DATEV integration for seamless tax preparation

### Collaboration Features

- **Multi-therapist Scheduling:** Shared calendars with resource allocation and availability management
- **Client Portal Access:** Self-service appointment booking, invoice viewing, and treatment history
