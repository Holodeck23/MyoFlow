# Product Mission (Lite)

MyoFlow is an Austrian therapy practice management system that helps massage therapists replace fragmented Excel/Word/WhatsApp workflows by providing an all-in-one cockpit with regulatory compliance and revenue optimization features.

MyoFlow serves solo Austrian massage therapists and small clinics who currently waste 2-3 hours daily on administrative tasks using disconnected tools. Unlike generic practice management software, MyoFlow provides Austria-specific regulatory compliance (Kleinunternehmerregelung, GDPR Article 9, VAT calculations) and therapist-optimized workflows that reduce training time from weeks to hours while increasing practice profitability by 15-25% through automated retention campaigns and revenue analytics.
