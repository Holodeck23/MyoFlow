## Legal & Tax Considerations

### Austrian VAT
- All prices exclude Austrian VAT (20%)
- B2B customers (therapists with UID): Reverse charge (no VAT added)
- B2C customers (if applicable): Price + 20% VAT

**Example:**
- Massage tier: €29 + €5.80 VAT = €34.80 (B2C)
- Massage tier: €29 (B2B with valid UID)

### Kleinunternehmer Exception
- Therapists below €55,000 annual revenue may be VAT-exempt
- MyoFlow must still charge VAT (B2B reverse charge still applies)
