# User Settings Dashboard - Lite Summary

Create a comprehensive settings dashboard that allows Austrian therapists to manage their professional profile, business information, service rates, and tax compliance settings in a centralized interface. This system streamlines practice management while ensuring Austrian regulatory compliance (VAT, Kleinunternehmer status, professional designations) and integrates with existing MyoFlow components to automatically populate business details and service rates across invoices, appointments, and client management.

## Key Points
- Centralized settings management for Austrian massage therapists
- Professional profile, business info, service rates, and tax compliance configuration
- Seamless integration with existing MyoFlow invoice and appointment systems
