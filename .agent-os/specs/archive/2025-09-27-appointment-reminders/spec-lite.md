# Appointment Reminders - Lite Summary

Implement automated email reminder system that reduces therapy appointment no-shows by up to 95% through timely, GDPR-compliant notifications sent to Austrian clients. System automatically sends German-language email reminders 24h and 2h before appointments with simple confirm/reschedule options, eliminating manual reminder calls and reducing administrative burden by 2+ hours daily.

## Key Points
- Automated GDPR-compliant email reminders (24h + 2h before appointments)
- German-language notifications with confirm/reschedule functionality
- Reduces no-shows by up to 95% and saves 2+ hours daily administration time
