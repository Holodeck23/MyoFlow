> Version: 1.0.0

## User Experience Design

### Austrian Medical Visual Identity
**Color Psychology & Cultural Resonance:**
- Primary Blue (#1565C0): Medical trust, Austrian institutional reliability
- Austrian Red (#C8102E): Cultural identity, accent for important actions
- Medical Gray Palette: Professional healthcare software aesthetic
- Success Green (#16A34A): Positive states, compliance indicators
- Warning Amber (#F59E0B): Attention states, Kleinunternehmer thresholds

**Typography Hierarchy:**
- Primary: Inter font family for medical software professionalism
- Display Scale: 32px/28px/24px for dashboard headers and hero content
- Body Scale: 16px/14px for forms and content with medical-grade readability
- Caption Scale: 12px for legal notices and compliance information
- Line Height: 1.5 for enhanced readability in medical contexts

### Dashboard Hero Experience
**Kleinunternehmer Progress Tracking:**
- Prominent dashboard card with visual progress bar
- Current revenue vs €35,000 threshold with percentage completion
- Color-coded states: Safe (green), Warning (amber), Exceeded (red)
- Austrian tax year context with reset indicators
- Quick action buttons for tax consultation and documentation

**Austrian Compliance Overview:**
- Visual status indicators for GDPR, VAT, business registration compliance
- Color-coded compliance dashboard with immediate action items
- Austrian legal requirement checklists with progress tracking
- Professional medical aesthetic with clear information hierarchy

### Mobile-First Responsive Patterns
**Touch-Optimized Interactions:**
- Minimum 44px touch targets for appointment scheduling
- Swipe gestures for client navigation and quick actions
- Bottom navigation for primary therapy practice workflows
- Pull-to-refresh patterns for appointment and client updates
- Thumb-friendly floating action buttons for common tasks

**Responsive Breakpoints:**
- Mobile: 375px-768px (primary Austrian therapist usage)
- Tablet: 768px-1024px (appointment scheduling, client management)
- Desktop: 1024px+ (administrative tasks, reporting)

## Interactive Design Patterns

### Austrian Form UX Patterns
**Address & Location Fields:**
- PLZ (postal code) autocomplete with Austrian postal validation
- Bundesland dropdown with all 9 Austrian states and cultural familiarity
- Street address formatting matching Austrian postal conventions
- UID number input with real-time Austrian business registration validation
- Professional form validation with helpful Austrian context

**GDPR Health Data Indicators:**
- Visual encryption indicators for sensitive health information
- Color-coded privacy levels with clear Austrian legal context
- Professional consent management with medical software aesthetic
- Audit trail visualization for compliance demonstration

### Navigation & Information Architecture
**Professional Medical Navigation:**
- Austrian flag accent elements in primary navigation
- Medical iconography with therapy practice context
- Breadcrumb system with Austrian business terminology
- Professional sidebar with Austrian compliance quick-access
- Context-aware navigation based on therapist workflow patterns

**Dashboard Information Hierarchy:**
- Hero section: Kleinunternehmer tracking and Austrian compliance status
- Secondary cards: Recent appointments, client alerts, revenue insights
- Quick actions: Austrian-specific tasks (VAT filing, insurance claims)
- Professional data visualization with medical software aesthetic

## Responsive Design Implementation

### Mobile Experience (375px-768px)
**Therapy Practice On-The-Go:**
- Single-column layout optimized for one-handed usage
- Bottom tab navigation for primary workflows (clients, appointments, invoices)
- Swipe-based client browsing with Austrian contact information
- Quick appointment actions with therapy-specific context
- Offline-first design for client data access during treatments

### Tablet Experience (768px-1024px)
**Appointment Management Interface:**
- Two-column layout for appointment scheduling with client details
- Split-screen client management with notes and health information
- Professional forms optimized for tablet input during consultations
- Austrian calendar integration with Bundesland-specific holidays
- Touch-optimized invoice generation with Austrian tax compliance

### Desktop Experience (1024px+)
**Administrative Dashboard:**
- Multi-column dashboard with comprehensive Austrian business overview
- Professional data tables for client management and appointment history
- Advanced Austrian compliance features and tax optimization tools
- Comprehensive settings management with business registration integration
- Full-screen Austrian invoice generation and PDF export capabilities

## Accessibility & Compliance Considerations

### WCAG Basic Compliance
- Color contrast ratios meeting AA standards for medical software
- Keyboard navigation patterns for all interactive elements
- Screen reader compatibility for Austrian business terminology
- Focus indicators with medical software professional aesthetic
- Alternative text for Austrian cultural visual elements

### Austrian Legal UX Requirements
- GDPR consent interfaces with clear Austrian legal language
- Professional medical data handling visual indicators
- Austrian business registration compliance visual cues
- VAT and tax information display with legal accuracy
- Professional error handling with Austrian business context
