# Therapist Profile Settings - Lite Summary

Implement a comprehensive therapist profile and settings system that allows therapists to manage their business information, service rate defaults, and Austrian compliance settings. This system will streamline invoice creation by pre-populating business details and service rates, while ensuring Austrian regulatory compliance for therapy practices. The settings page centralizes business profile management, service rate templates, and Austrian compliance configuration, automatically integrating with the existing PDF invoice generation system.

## Key Points
- Centralized therapist profile management with Austrian business compliance fields
- Service rate defaults and templates for streamlined invoice creation
- Austrian regulatory settings integration with existing PDF generation system
